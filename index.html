About

Stacy is the visionary of Kalos Artwork & Words, LLC. "Kalos" is an ancient Greek word which means what is beautiful and noble. In her work, Stacy's goal is to reflect works and designs that live up to the connotation of what is beautiful and noble, with a taste of excellence. The company has two branches- artwork and print. With the artwork branch, Stacy creates commissioned art pieces such as paintings, drawings, book cover designs as well as logo designs. Print products include her self-published novels and original inspirational pieces. "Destiny's Edge: A Journey to Reclaim a Stolen Legacy" is her first self-published novel. Stacy's academic backgroud includes an Associates Degree in General Studies, a Bachelor of Science Degree in Sociology, Master's Degree coursework in Social Work. The entreprenuer is currently working on an Associate in Graphic Design Degree at Brightpoint Community College. Stacy's theme scripture she uses for her business comes from Proverbs 16:18 (KJV), "A man's gift maketh room for him, and bringeth him before great men."
